# Upkyp Rental Property Management Platform


UPKYP is a centralized platform designed to streamline property and rental management by bringing essential workflows into one secure and structured system.

The platform supports landlords, tenants, and administrators by providing clear visibility into leases, billing, payments, and property operations, reducing manual work and improving coordination between all parties.

UPKYP focuses on:
- Organized property and unit management
- Structured lease agreements and approvals
- Transparent billing and payment tracking
- Secure communication and notifications
- Scalable administration and oversight

The system is built to support growth, enforce consistency in processes, and maintain data integrity across all rental operations.

UPKYP is continuously evolving, with an emphasis on reliability, security, and user-centered workflows.
